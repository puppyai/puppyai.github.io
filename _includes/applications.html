Industrial applications

Industrial applications of visual recognition that could be made dead simple:
  • RETAIL: People/traffic counting without double counting.
  • MEDICINE: Visual pattern recognition and diagnosis of the nature of certain tumors; robotic surgeries
  • SECURITY: People/traffic counting without double counting.
  • RETAIL: Anomaly detection; real time object labeling
  • AGRICULTURE: Drone view and assessment of farmland patches; accurate identification of plant disease foci and areas differing in disease severity in fields.
